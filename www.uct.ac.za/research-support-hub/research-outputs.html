Related information

Digital Collections at UCT: Ibali
When it comes to collections with a wide variety of media and interrelated content, the Ibali platform (combination of Omeka S  and IIIF) is a most suitable place to showcase them. Ibali allows for a customisable user experience through the creation of narratives using digital media.

Digital Preservation at UCT: Izolo
Izolo, the new UCT-wide digital preservation service, ensures that digital files created today can be found and used tomorrow. UCT Libraries have launched Izolo, running on Arkivum Perpetua Usability and Preservation modules, to provide business-process driven, structured data preservation workflows for the entire campus community.
